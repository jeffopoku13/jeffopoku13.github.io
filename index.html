About
Welcome to the Center for Research In Information Technology (CRIT) website. The CRIT is a Research Center that provides high quality research in Information Technology field.

The CRIT provides an place for students to and work on advancing their knowledge in understanding software applications and further their tech skills to create a better future.

  • Information Technology

  • Data Science
    CRIT performs extensive research in the area of Data Science to provide analytical solutions in the area of nuclear and cybersecurity to federal / state governments and national labs. Current research is focused on Machine Learning, Data Analytics and Visualization.
